About

Block Insights provides real-time tracking and analysis of large transactions, making pending transaction (aka. mempool) data freely accessible to ecosystem.

Every second, thousands of new transactions appear in the mempool

  • Our alert service traces transactions across the Bitcoin blockchain over an entire transaction's lifecycle, from inception to finality.

Features

    Unmatched per-event resolution data on mempool transactions

    • Receive in-flight event data on any transaction before it is confirmed on-chain.

    Monitor the mempool with instant updates for every transaction state change, error sent to your Slack, Discord, and more

    • Customize alerts based on market price, volume, ETH gas fees, and Bitcoin mempool size.

Upcoming Releases

Custom alerts based on:

  • Volume, Wallet, ETH gas fees, and Mempool size.

Coverage for other transaction status including:

  • Pending – a new transaction was detected in mempool is eligible for inclusion in a block
  • Failed – mined transaction failed to execute due to an EVM error.
  • Stuck - A transaction was detected in the mempool Queued area but is not eligible for inclusion in a block.
